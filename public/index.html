Our Mission
To create a world where every pet is safe, cherished, and deeply connected to their human family. With PetsieIQ, you're not just signing up for an app; you're joining a movement to protect and empower pets across the nation. And because it's free, every pet, regardless of their owner's circumstances, can benefit from our cutting-edge technology. Together, we're making our world a safer place for them.
We aim to:
  • Reunite lost and stolen pets
  • Protect every pet with unique facial ID technology
  • Support you with personalised tips from your AI companion
  • Reward our community with exclusive perks and events
Care they feel, Safety you trust.
